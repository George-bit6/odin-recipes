Hummus

Description

A quick and simple step-by-step recipe to make the traditional Hummus spread we all know and love takes no more than an hour and a half to make!!

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup of chickpeas
  • Half a cup of Tahini
  • Two Lemons
  • 2 tablespoons of Olive oil
  • Half a cup of ice cubes
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Place already rinsed chickpeas in a pot filled with water and boil them at medium heat for about an hour until they become soft
  2. Fitler the chickpeas to remove all the water
  3. Place the chickpeas in a blender
  4. Add the Tahini, lemon, olive oil, salt (according to your liking), and ice cubes, then blend them until they form a smooth paste
